LORENTZ SYMMETRY AS THE BOOST SQUEEZE
Spin(3,1)

A Lorentz boost is a change to a moving frame, and on a grid of space against
time it is a squeeze. It stretches one diagonal of the light cone and shrinks the
other by the matching amount, leaving the two cone lines themselves in place. The
squeeze is the same diagonal factor a from the Iwasawa decomposition of item 08,
now read with the two axes being space and time. The thing the boost holds fixed
is the light cone, and that fixed cone is the arrival.

================================================================
PROCESS  -  squeeze along the cone diagonals
================================================================

The grid is drawn with space across and time up, and the two diagonals are the
light cone, the paths of light through the origin. A boost slides points along
the hyperbolas between the diagonals: one diagonal direction is stretched, the
other squeezed, while the diagonals stay put.

   t                              t
   |   \         /                |   \           /
   |    \       /                 |    \       o-'      stretched this way
   |     \     /        boost     |     \    o'
 --+------*----+-- x    ---->   --+------*----+-- x
   |     /     \                  |    o'\     \
   |    /       \                 |  o'   \     \        squeezed this way
   |   /         \                | '      \     \

  the two diagonals are the light cone (paths of light); they do not move.
  a point off the cone slides along a hyperbola, out along one diagonal and in
  along the other.

================================================================
ARRIVAL  -  the fixed cone and the invariant
================================================================

The arrival is the light cone held fixed, and with it the quantity that does not
change under the squeeze, which is space squared minus time squared. In the
light-cone coordinates u = x + t and v = x - t the boost multiplies u by a factor
and divides v by the same factor, so the product u times v, which is space
squared minus time squared, is left alone. This is the area-one stretch of item
08 acting on spacetime, and the manifest sources the Lorentz symmetry to
derivations D14 and D15. To redraw it by hand, draw the two light-cone diagonals,
pick a point between them, and slide it out along one diagonal and in along the
other so that it stays on its hyperbola.
